Search operators

key:value Matches if key contains value. Supported keys: title, artist, albumartist, album.
e.g. artist:lunceford
before/after:year e.g. after:2015
negation e.g. -album:live, will exlude albums containing "live"
>/< for BPM and year e.g. bpm>200 or year<2010
words (no operators) will be matched against title, artist, albumartist, album
